Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts in a university auditorium. Its initial aim was to foster knowledge sharing and collaboration within the burgeoning tech community. Over the years, it rapidly grew in popularity, attracting speakers and attendees from across the nation.

By 2015, TechCon had become a flagship event, moving to larger venues and expanding its scope to include international speakers and a wider array of topics, from software development to emerging hardware innovations. Each year, we strive to build upon our legacy of excellence and community.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the global tech community by providing an unparalleled platform for learning, networking, and innovation. We aim to:

  • Educate: Deliver cutting-edge content and insights from industry leaders.
  • Connect: Facilitate meaningful connections among professionals, students, and enthusiasts.
  • Inspire: Spark new ideas and encourage breakthroughs in technology.
  • Innovate: Showcase the latest advancements and foster future technological growth.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has been graced by some of the brightest minds in technology. Here are a few who have left a lasting impact:

Dr. Ava Chen

Dr. Chen is a pioneer in the field of Artificial Intelligence, renowned for her work on ethical AI development and machine learning algorithms. Her keynote at TechCon 2018 on "AI for Good" was highly influential.

Mr. Ben Carter

A cybersecurity expert and author, Mr. Carter shared invaluable insights on data privacy and advanced threat detection at TechCon 2020. His practical advice continues to shape industry best practices.

Ms. Chloe Davis

Ms. Davis, a leading voice in sustainable technology, presented on "Green Computing" at TechCon 2022, advocating for environmentally responsible tech solutions and practices.
